A simple and structured process

We keep things clear and straightforward so you always know what to expect. Each step is designed to be transparent, respectful, and easy to follow.

Introduction

We start with a confidential conversation to understand your situation, your business, and whether selling is something you are considering now or later.

Analysis and valuation

We review your operations, customer base, and financials to get a clear picture of the business and determine a fair value.

Proposal

If there is a good fit, we present a simple and clear proposal outlining the structure, price, and possible transition options.

Deal closing

We work through the details together to finalize the agreement while keeping the process smooth and low stress.

Bring your shop to the next level

After closing, we focus on strengthening the business — supporting the team, improving operations, and building on the reputation you created.
